The Board of Education has approved guidelines that all students must follow when using computer systems in the Los Angeles Unified School District. These guidelines identify acceptable - or allowable - uses of the computer network and Internet, and also clarify unacceptable – or inappropriate – uses. These guidelines help keep students safe.

Acceptable Uses
  • All parents/guardians must provide a signed page acknowledging these guidelines each year; schools will keep a copy on file
  • Parents/Guardians or the District may deny computer privileges if students do not follow these guidelines.
  • Students who leave our District will no longer be able to use the system.
  • All students must follow the guidelines and report any misuse of computers systems or use of the Internet to a teacher, aide or administrator.
  • Access to the network and Internet is for school projects and work only.
Unacceptable Uses
Violating Laws
  • downloading or sending pornography of any kind, obscene pictures, harmful materials, materials that encourage others to break laws, private information or copyrighted materials
  • selling or buying illegal items or substances
  • using anonymous email sites, spamming or spreading viruses
Causing Harm to Others
  • using profane or rude language; threatening, harassing, or making untrue statements
  • looking at, sending or downloading offensive, harassing or negative materials
  • deleting, copying, changing or forging other users’ names, emails, files or data
  • hiding your identity, pretending to be someone else, or sending anonymous emails
  • damaging equipment, files, data or the network in any way
  • hacking or attempting to access private information
  • downloading and accessing chain letters
  • using another student’s password or username; sharing passwords with others
  • preventing others from accessing their accounts
Using Internet for Commercial Purposes
  • using the Internet to buy or sell items, or to fundraise
  • using the Internet for religious purposes or personal politics
Safety
  • Outside school, students may only access LAUSD net accounts under parent/guardian supervision.
  • Do not reveal personal information about yourself or another on the Internet; do not provide your name, home address, phone number or photographs of yourself or others.
  • Do not meet in person anyone you have met on the Internet.
Discipline
  • Misuse will result in limited use or removal of your account.
  • Misuse may lead to suspension, expulsion or criminal prosecution.
