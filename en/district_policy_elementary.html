These guidelines help students learn what they can and cannot do when using computers in our District. These guidelines will also help keep students safe.

Guidelines
  • Your parents /guardians must sign a form each year before you can use computers.
  • If you do not use computers correctly, your parents/guardians or the school will not let you use them.
  • Tell a teacher or the principal if another student is not using computers safely.
  • Use our computers only for your schoolwork or homework.
You May Not:
  • Find, look at or send bad pictures
  • Sell or buy items
  • "Spam" or spread computer viruses
  • Use bad language; say things about others that are unkind
  • Use another studentâ€™s name, password or email
  • Pretend to be someone else
  • Hurt or damage computers in any way
Safety
  • When away from school, you may only access our programs if an adult is with you.
  • Never give your name, address, phone number or send pictures to anyone on the Internet.
  • Never meet anyone you met on the Internet; always tell an adult if someone tries to meet you.
  • You may not use computers if you break these rules.
  • There may be other consequences for not using the system correctly including suspension or expulsion
Always be SAFE....RESPONSIBLE....RESPECTFUL
