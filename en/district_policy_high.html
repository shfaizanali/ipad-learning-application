The Board of Education has approved guidelines that all students must follow when using computer systems in the Los Angeles Unified School District. These guidelines identify acceptable - or allowable - uses of the computer network and Internet, and also clarify unacceptable or inappropriate uses. These guidelines are designed to keep students safe. Remember: the use of the District computer system is a privilege not a right.

Acceptable Uses
  • All students must provide a signed page acknowledging this AUP each year. Students under age 18 must have their parents or guardians sign; schools will keep a copy on file.
  • Parents/Guardians or school district may revoke student access to the Internet if the policy has been violated.
  • Students who leave LAUSD lose network privileges.
  • All users must follow the AUP and report any misuse of the network or Internet to a teacher, supervisor or administrator.
  • Access to the network and Internet is for educational uses only.
Unacceptable Uses
Violating Laws
  • accessing or sending pornography of any kind, obscene pictures or depictions, harmful materials, materials that encourage others to violate the law, confidential information or copyrighted materials
  • selling or purchasing illegal items or substances
  • obtaining or using anonymous email sites, spamming or spreading viruses
Causing Harm to Others
  • using profanity or impolite language; threatening, harassing, or making false statements
  • accessing, sending or downloading offensive, harassing or negative materials
  • deleting, copying, changing or forging others users’ names, emails, files or data
  • disguising one's identity, pretending to be someone else, sending anonymous emails
  • damaging equipment, files, data or the network in any way
  • hacking or attempting to access information protected by privacy laws
  • downloading and accessing chain letters or pyramid schemes
  • using another student’s account password or username or sharing passwords with others
  • preventing others from accessing their accounts
Using Internet for Commercial Purposes
  • using the Internet for financial gain, personal advertising, or conducting for-profit business
  • engaging in fundraising, solicitation for religious purposes or personal politics
Internet Safety
  • Outside school, students under age 18 may only access LAUSD net accounts under parent/guardian supervision
  • Never reveal personal information about yourself or another on the Internet; never reveal your name, home address, phone number or photographs of yourself or others.
  • Never meet in person anyone you have met on the Internet.
Penalties
  • Misuse will result in restriction or cancellation of your account.
  • Misuse may lead to disciplinary and/or legal action including suspension, expulsion or criminal prosecution
Disclaimer

  • is not responsible for the accuracy or quality of information obtained through Internet use
